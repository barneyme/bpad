Welcome to bPad!

bPad is a simple productivity suite with basic word processor, spreadsheet and sticky notes apps. bPad is completely private and your work never leaves your computer. bPad automatically saves your work to your browser. You can close the tab and your content will be here when you return! bPad can be installed as a PWA (Progressive Web App) with supported web browsers.

Spreadsheet üìä

  • Click on any cell to select it. The formula bar at the top will show its content.
  • To create a formula, start with an equals sign (=).
    • Basic Math: Perform calculations with multiple cells. Examples:
      • Addition: =A1+B1+C1
      • Subtraction: =A1-B1
      • Multiplication: =A1*B1*C1
      • Division: =A1/B1
    • SUM Function: Add cells and ranges together.
      • =SUM(A1:A10, C5)
  • Press Enter in the formula bar to see the result.
