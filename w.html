Welcome to bPad (w)

This is a single-page wiki that works entirely in your browser. All data is stored locally on your device.

To get started:

  • Click "New" to create a new wiki page
  • Click "Edit" to modify the current page
  • Use [[Page Name]] syntax to create links to other pages

This wiki supports basic markdown:

  • **bold text**
  • *italic text*
  • # Heading 1, ## Heading 2, etc.
  • - List items
  • [Link text](https://example.com)
